Last updated: March 4, 2026

1. General Provisions

This document defines the procedure for storing and protecting personal data of Nubiotica.kz users.

2. Composition of Processed Data

The Operator processes the following categories of user data provided through forms on the site:

  • Full Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Telegram messenger nickname

3. Purposes of Data Storage

Collected data is stored and processed strictly to fulfill the following purposes:

  • Processing incoming applications, requests, and inquiries;
  • Communication with users (consulting, technical support);
  • Informing about products, order statuses, or pre-orders.

4. Storage and Protection Conditions

Personal data is stored exclusively in electronic form on secure servers in compliance with the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

The Operator takes a set of legal, organizational, and technical measures to protect personal data from unauthorized or accidental access, copying, distribution, as well as from other unlawful actions by third parties.

5. Access to Data

Only authorized employees of the Operator, whose job duties require working with such data, have access to users' personal data. Transferring data to third parties is only possible in cases expressly provided by law.

6. Storage Periods

Personal data is stored until the purposes of its processing are achieved, or until the user revokes consent for processing, unless otherwise provided by legislation.

7. Data Deletion

If the processing purposes are achieved or upon a written request from the user (revocation of consent), personal data is subject to immediate destruction (deletion) from the Operator's databases.

8. Contacts for Inquiries

To revoke consent to data processing, as well as for any questions regarding the storage and protection of personal information, users can contact: hello@nubiotica.kz.
